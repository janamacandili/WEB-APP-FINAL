ABOUT GROOVE BOULEVARD

HISTORY

Established in March 2025, Groove Boulevard was primarily created with the clear objective of supporting the local music scene by providing essential instruments and performance gear for aspiring artists and bands, particularly in urban communities across Southern Luzon and Central Visayas. As the passion for music among the youth and grassroots creatives grew, so did the demand for accessible, affordable, and trustworthy sources of musical instruments and services. In response, Groove Boulevard expanded its vision—offering a complete line of instruments, sound equipment, and creative support to musicians of all levels. Through this initiative, Groove Boulevard became a growing force in promoting musical expression and community-driven creativity.

In April 2025, Groove Boulevard launched its first experience center in Nuvali, Laguna with only 10 dedicated individuals—six customer care staff and four operations personnel. The space featured a curated showroom of musical instruments, a collaborative jamming zone, and a series of beginner-friendly workshops and open mic nights. Due to overwhelming support and increasing demand, Groove Boulevard opened its second branch in Cebu City by the end of the same year.

Today, Groove Boulevard continues to thrive—employing over 80 team members, collaborating with over 40 independent artists and music groups, and managing 10 retail and performance hubs across the Philippines. The brand has also expanded its catalog to include international and homegrown instrument brands, music production tools, and community engagement programs. Groove Boulevard's success is driven by its inclusive leadership, consistent product quality, passionate staff, and unyielding support for Filipino music culture. With eyes on the future, Groove Boulevard remains committed to uplifting the next generation of music creators and audiences nationwide.

VALUES

In its early years of service, Groove Boulevard has consistently operated as a customer-centered organization, upholding its core value of placing passion, creativity, and satisfaction of its community above profit. Groove Boulevard deeply values building genuine and lasting relationships with musicians, artists, and customers by offering attentive guidance, open collaboration, and personalized support in helping them find what best suits their artistic journey.

MISSION

Our mission is to cultivate and empower the creative spirit that stems from a deep love for music. With this purpose, Groove Boulevard is committed to expanding its reach by establishing more hubs and partnerships across the country. Beyond growth, we aim to support musicians and creators by offering access to top-quality, innovative instruments and tools—ensuring that every artist, regardless of background, is equipped to express their passion to the fullest.

VISION

Since launching in the Philippine music scene, Groove Boulevard—together with its passionate and welcoming team—has always envisioned itself as a strong advocate for local musical talent and a premier destination for high-quality yet affordable instruments and gear. With a growing selection of trusted brands and creative services, Groove Boulevard strives to become a go-to haven for music lovers, performers, and creators from all walks of life.
